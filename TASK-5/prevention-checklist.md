Prevention on phishing attacks : 


Preventing phishing attacks requires awareness, caution, and the use of basic security practices. One of the most effective methods is to always verify the source of emails or messages before clicking on any links.
Users should carefully check the sender’s email address and avoid messages that create urgency or panic.

It is important to never share sensitive information such as passwords, OTPs, or bank details through email, SMS, or unknown websites.
Legitimate organizations never ask for such details in this manner. Before entering any personal information, users should ensure that the website URL is correct and begins with “https://”⁠�.

Another key prevention method is enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts like banking, email, and social media. 
This adds an extra layer of security even if login credentials are compromised.
